What We Do

Tree Services

From complete removals and storm damage remediation to pruning, stump grinding, lot clearing, and risk assessments, Barton's Tree Care handles tree work with licensed expertise and clean execution.

Tree Removal

Complete tree removal for hazardous, unwanted, storm-damaged, or difficult trees.

Storm Damaged Tree Remediation

Experienced remediation for storm-damaged trees that have fallen on structures or are creating a safety risk.

Pruning

All phases of tree pruning with an emphasis on preserving the health, safety, and structure of your trees.

Stump Grinding

Expert below-grade stump and root removal to properly clear the area after tree work.

Lot Clearing

Selective clearing that preserves healthy mature trees when possible. Full lot clearing also available.

Risk Assessment

Free tree risk assessments to help identify hazardous trees before they become bigger problems.

Planting

Large and small-scale planting projects for residential properties and landscape improvements.

Construction Management Plans

Detailed planning and implementation of tree management during all phases of construction.

Fertilization & Pest Control

Tree health support through fertilization and pest-control services.

Cabling & Bracing

Structural support for trees that may need reinforcement to improve safety and stability.
